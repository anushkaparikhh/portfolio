Sensescape X Microsoft

Summary

Sensecape X Microsoft is an immersive conference hosted at Microsoft Garage in NYC↗ that gathers artists, designers, technologists, and thinkers reimagining interaction across disciplines, bodies, and realities.
“Beyond Boundaries: Artifact of the Future,” invites you to engage with works that resist categorization—hybrid objects, speculative systems, and sensory architectures that stretch the limits of medium, tool, and touch. These are not just experiments; they are artifacts of tomorrow’s archive.
The branding emulates this inquisive quality, blending together the mechanism imagery of nature and creative technology, and centers the intersection of innovation and archive. It is applied to exhibition design, VR website, social media design, and event ephemera. [5 months]
